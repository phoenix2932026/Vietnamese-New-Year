    Tet Viet in Ottawa

    Vietnamese Lunar New Year is also celebrated by the Vietnamese community in Ottawa. Even though Canada has a cold winter, people still keep their traditions alive. Families gather together, enjoy traditional food, wear Ao Dai, and take part in cultural activities. Community events are often organized to bring people together and help younger generations learn about Vietnamese culture.
