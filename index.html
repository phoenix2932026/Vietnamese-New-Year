Welcome to Tet Nguyen Dan

Tet Nguyen Dan is the most important traditional holiday in Vietnam. It is a time for family reunion, respect for ancestors, and welcoming the new year with happiness and hope. Tet isn’t just a date on the calendar. It’s a feeling. It’s the frantic rush to clean the house, the vibrant yellow of apricot blossoms, and most importantly, the overwhelming abundance of food. If you ever find yourself in a Vietnamese home during this time, you’ll realize that we don’t just “eat”; we celebrate our history and our hopes for the future through a specific set of Tet dishes.

Explore Vietnamese Lunar New Year

The purpose of this website is to introduce the Vietnamese New Year and help people understand its traditions, food, clothing, and activities. It also shows that Tet Nguyen Dan is a unique Vietnamese celebration with its own customs and cultural values. Moreover, this website helps people understand that the Lunar New Year is not only Chinese New Year. While many other countries celebrate the Lunar New Year, such as Vietnam, Singapore, Malaysia, Korea, and Indonesia, etc. Each country has its own traditions and cultural meanings. This website aims to share Vietnamese culture and help others better understand the uniqueness of Tet in Vietnam.
